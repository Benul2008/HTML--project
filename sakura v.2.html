Our Story

Discover the passion and tradition behind Sakura Japanese Restaurant

Founded in 1995 by Hiroshi Tanaka, Sakura began as a small 10-seat restaurant in Tokyo's Ginza district. Chef Tanaka's vision was to bring authentic Japanese flavors to the world while maintaining the highest standards of quality and presentation.

After winning multiple awards in Japan, we expanded internationally, opening our first overseas location in 2005. Today, we continue the tradition of excellence with Chef Aiko Nakamura at the helm, who trained under Chef Tanaka for 15 years.

Our ingredients are sourced daily from Tokyo's famous Tsukiji Market and local suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability and quality. We believe in the Japanese philosophy of "shun" - serving ingredients at their peak of freshness.

Chef Aiko Nakamura
